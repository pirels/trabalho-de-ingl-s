about the documentary

"Money, Explained" is a Netflix documentary series released in 2021, produced by Vox Media, that clarifies aspects of personal and collective finances, such as debts, frauds, retirement, casinos, and credit cards. With five episodes, the series uses colorful animations and interviews to simplify complex concepts. Narration by different personalities, such as Tiffany Haddish and Bobby Cannavale, makes the content more dynamic and accessible.

Strengths

Accessibility and Clarity

The series manages to explain financial topics that are usually difficult to understand in a and engaging way. The animations and fluid narrative make the content accessible even to those who have no previous knowledge of finance.

Diversity of Topics

The coverage of various aspects of personal and collective finance provides a comprehensive and educational overview. Each episode focuses on a specific topic, allowing for a deeper and more detailed exploration of each subject.

Financial Education

"Money, Explained" has a strong educational component, encouraging viewers to reflect on their own financial practices and make more informed decisions. It is a useful tool for promoting financial literacy at a time when many people are facing economic difficulties.

Conclusion


"Money, Explained" is a valuable documentary series for those who want to better understand the world of finance in an accessible and engaging way. Although it may be superficial at times, it fulfills its main objective of educating and informing the public about critical financial issues. It is a production that mixes entertainment and education, making complex themes more digestible and stimulating a necessary reflection on how we deal with money in our lives.
